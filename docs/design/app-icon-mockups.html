Spuk · App Icon

A ghost that speaks.

Right now Spuk ships with no app icon, so it shows the blank default on the Dock, in Finder, and on Windows. These three directions give it a real identity — built from the brand's Aurora purple and the one idea unique to Spuk: a ghost whose wisps form a voice waveform, because the app turns your voice into text.
